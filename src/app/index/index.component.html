Powerful procurement and Inventory Management under one roof

Success in e-commerce requires robust inventory management practices. Jennifer let’s you automate, streamline and centralize stock operations to provide your business the control and insight needed for scaling growth and profitability.

Process transactions with minimal effort

Speed up your post sale processes with minimal effort. Automated emails, purchase orders, sale invoices, customer payments, and all other activities can be done swiftly under one roof.

Stock transfer

Automatically pass the Sales and Purchases entries to the respective locations in different states by enabling inter unit transfer under the new GST regime.

Automatic Replenishment triggers

Determine how much stock needs to be reordered effortlessly. Preset minimum, maximum, and reorder points for each item that you want to be replenished using historical data. Create purchase orders when inventory levels for those items approach their reorder levels.

Inwarding Automation

Manage end to end procurement and shipment of inventory to Amazon. Keep a track of the inventory purchased and real time stock levels across different SKUs.

Inventory warehouse management made ridiculously easy

No more losing of customers due to disorganized order management. Track and synchronize data of all stock transfers from across sources so you never have to run the risk of disappointing your costumes with a "Sold Out" Message.

Reduce Pilferage

Track inventory on a real time basis to check what gets lost in transit during delivery. If Amazon processes a ‘refund’ to the customer and the inventory doesn’t get returned, Jennifer will provide full data to raise a claim to Amazon.

Boost profitability

Manage unfulfillable inventory by categorizing them into different grades in order to sell the best stock at a higher price and the dead ones at a lower price.

Direct sync with Tally

Automatically synchronize your invoices, purchase orders and bill to liquidators between Jennifer and Tally.

Multi user level access

Give access to your team members within your organisation with different roles and module permissions. Delegate work without losing control.

Real-time end to end reconciliation

Jennifer automates reconciliation process in real-time for amazon charges, stock refunds, unsaleable, lost or damaged inventory.

Keep a tab on Amazon charges

Automatically analyze millions of shipments and check if Amazon is charging you as per the terms for storage and delivery of shipments.

Automatic classification of inventory

Monitor changes in inventory levels when orders are fulfilled by Amazon and automatically classify them into different categories such as returned, damaged or lost in transit.

Track inventory returns and amazon refunds in a snapshot

  • Get a summary of unsaleable receivables from Amazon
  • Monitor inventory returns or refunds and file claims in time in case of any errors
  • Avoid heavy losses by getting a complete overview of inventory

Simplify the process for GST returns filings across all states of operations on Amazon.

  • Record all GST inputs such as purchases, expenses of Amazon charges and outputs such as sales and promotional activities
  • Get complete GST filings in formats as prescribed by the government (3, 3A, 3B)
  • Download returns and upload directly on GST portal
